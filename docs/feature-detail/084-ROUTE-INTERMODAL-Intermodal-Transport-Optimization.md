## Purpose

Optimize multi-modal transport (truck → ship → truck) for carbon efficiency.

---

## Intermodal Options

| Route | Modes | Carbon Benefit |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| Osaka → Hamburg | Truck + Ship | -40% vs all truck |
| Tokyo → LA | Ship + Truck | -30% vs all air |
| Japan → EU | Rail + Ship | -35% vs air |
